Powercoders

Powercoders is an organization dedicated to helping people with immigrant status in integrating in the IT market.

Who are Powercoders

"Impacting Lives by Teaching Digital Skills" is powercoders' motto. It is a non-profit organization created to help the less fortunate get the opportunity to experience, study and work in various tech jobs.

History of powercoders

powercoders started when the founder Christian Hirsig took a trip to the U.S and there met two people one with a cooking school for refugees and one with a coding academy for women. Inspired by them he came up with the idea of powercoders and founded the first powercoders program in Bern, Switzerland in 2017.

Locations

Currently powercoders operate in three countries.

  • Italy
  • Spain
  • Switzerland
